CORPORATE DUEL

Where strategy meets rivalry - battle it out in the ultimate Corporate Duel!

Description:

Corporate Duel is a high-energy business strategy competition where teams go head-to-head, testing their analytical skills, decision-making, and problem-solving abilities. Through a series of real-world inspired scenarios, participants experience the challenges of corporate strategy, negotiation, and leadership. It is not just a contest of rivalry but also a practical learning platform that equips young minds with an entrepreneurial mindset, teamwork, and critical thinking skills. The event encourages participants to think under pressure, analyze multiple perspectives, and craft sustainable solutions preparing them to face the dynamic corporate world with confidence.

CAPITAL QUEST

Where strategy meets rivalry - battle it out in the ultimate Corporate Duel!

Description:

Capital Quest is a simulated investment challenge designed to give participants first-hand exposure to the world of venture funding. Teams present their innovative business ideas to a panel of evaluators, working through multiple rounds that emphasize idea validation, value creation, and persuasive pitching. With mentoring opportunities and constructive feedback, the event helps participants refine their entrepreneurial vision and prepares them to confidently navigate real-life fundraising and investor engagement.Beyond competition, Capital Quest cultivates a deeper understanding of how startups secure capital, teaching the art of balancing creativity with financial sustainability.
